Research Interests

My personal research interests include radio astronomy, exploring the universe at radio wavelengths to study galaxy formation, active galactic nuclei, and interstellar phenomena. To support this and other research, I focus heavily on writing software for astronomy to develop robust, open-source tools tailored specifically for astronomical data processing. Additionally, I am passionate about leveraging and implementing general artificial intelligence applications (especially classical machine learning techniques) in science and engineering to solve problems and aid in research.
